What is this?

A citizen-maintained, non-partisan public ledger. Every promise in a government's manifesto is tracked against available public records. No party affiliation. No funding from political entities.

How we classify

Fulfilled — implemented. In Progress / Partial — work begun. Evaded — denied or reversed. Pending — no action. See methodology →
